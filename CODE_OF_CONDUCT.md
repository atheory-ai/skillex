# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects participants to be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- giving actionable, technical feedback
- assuming good intent while still being rigorous
- welcoming contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels
- focusing discussion on the code, design, or process rather than the person

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks, insults, or deliberately hostile conduct
- publishing private information without permission
- bad-faith disruption of project discussions or reviews

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other repository-managed communication.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer at `jeremy@atheory.ai`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled in good faith.
